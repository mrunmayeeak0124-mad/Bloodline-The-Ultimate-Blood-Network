🧠 Fear to Fearless

Breaking myths and building confidence in blood donation

❌ Myth: Blood donation is very painful

✅ Reality: Only a small needle prick lasting 2–3 seconds. Most donors describe it as a mild pinch.

❌ Myth: I will feel weak for days

✅ Fact: Body replaces fluids within 24 hours. Proper hydration prevents weakness.

❌ Myth: Blood donation reduces immunity

✅ Truth: No impact on immune system. Blood cells regenerate quickly.

❌ Myth: Only rare blood groups matter

✅ Reality: Every blood group is needed daily in hospitals.

💬 Frequently Asked Questions

Can I donate if I take BP/thyroid medicines?

Yes, if condition is controlled. Always inform the doctor before donation.

What if I feel dizzy?

Very rare. Staff are trained and provide immediate care like rest and hydration.

How often can I donate?

Every 3 months (men) and 4 months (women), depending on health.
