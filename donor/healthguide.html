🍎 Before Donation (24 Hours)

  • Eat iron-rich foods (spinach, lentils, dates)
  • Drink 500ml extra water before donation
  • Avoid oily/fried foods
  • Sleep at least 7–8 hours

📄 WHO Pre-Donation Guide

🩸 During Donation

  • Relax and stay calm
  • Procedure takes 8–10 minutes
  • Medical staff monitors vitals
  • Safe and sterile equipment used

💧 After Donation (First 24 Hours)

  • Drink 3–4 glasses of water
  • Eat protein & iron (eggs, nuts)
  • Avoid gym/heavy work for 24h
  • Take rest if dizzy

❓ Common Questions

Can I exercise after donation?

Avoid heavy lifting for 6–8 hours. Light activity is fine.

Will I feel weak?

Most donors feel normal. Eat and hydrate properly.

Can I donate if I am on medication?

Depends on condition. Always consult doctor.

🌟 Long-Term Donor Health

  • Maintain iron levels regularly
  • Donate every 3–4 months safely
  • Regular donors often have better health monitoring
"Healthy donors create a stronger healthcare system."
