About BloodLine

Smart matchmaking for blood donors & recipients — because every minute counts.

real‑time compatibility

BloodLine is a smart blood matchmaking platform that connects voluntary blood donors with patients in need. Our mission is to ensure the right blood reaches the right person at the right time.

We use advanced compatibility matching, real-time availability, and location-based search to make the blood donation process fast, reliable, and efficient.

Trusted by 40+ hospitals & blood banks.

connecting lives, one drop at a time

How it Works

Three simple steps to match life‑saving blood

1

Request / Register

Patient or hospital submits a blood request, or a donor registers with blood type & location.

2

Smart Match

Our algorithm finds compatible donors based on blood type, Rh factor, and proximity.

3

Connect & Save

Both parties are notified instantly. The donor is directed to the nearest collection point.
