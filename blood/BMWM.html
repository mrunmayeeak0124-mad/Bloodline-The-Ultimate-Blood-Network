BMWM

(Biomedical Waste Management)

Yellow Category

Items: Soiled waste, expired blood bags, anatomical waste, and gauze contaminated with blood.

Incineration

Red Category

Items: Contaminated plastic waste, recycled sets, tubing, gloves, and urine bags (no needles).

Autoclaving / Shredding

White Category

Items: Waste sharps including needles, syringes with fixed needles, and discarded scalpels.

Sterilization & Foundry

Blue Category

Items: Glassware, broken slides, medicine vials, and metallic body implants.

Chemical Disinfection
⚠️
Emergency Protocol: All blood spills must be treated with 10% Sodium Hypochlorite for 30 minutes before cleaning to ensure total decontamination.
